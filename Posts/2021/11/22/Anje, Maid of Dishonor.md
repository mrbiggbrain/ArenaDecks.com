## Overview

Anje is all about sucking the life out of your opponent, a slow bleeding style of deck that looks to capatalise on blood tokens to drain the opponent out while keeping your life total high.

Anje cares about vampires, so we care about vampires as well. 24 vampires call this deck their home, and each one ensures we stay agressive and dedicaed to our plan.
